What is the vision of FreeGarageBandLoops.com?

The vision of this website is to provide the largest variety of accessible, cool, and free loops (aka free samples or free audio files) so that musicians everywhere can enjoy the thrill of making music!

"What if I don't have GarageBand? Can I use Acid, FL Studio, or other sequencing software to use the free music loops on this website?"

Absolutely, yes! The loops on this site are just files containing sound, so you can use them with other commerical sequencing programs, like Acid or FL Studio (aka Fruity Loops).

And if you don't have commercial sequencing software and don't want to buy any, then you can use free/open source software sequencers like MuseScore, which works on Mac, Windows, & Linux platforms! It also doubles as free notation software!

 

Will this site get updated with new loops in the future!

Yes! I'm working on uploading more loops every week.

 

Can I make my own loops and share them?

Yes! Go here to e-mail us your loop(s), which you will be credited for.
