Why do we provide free GarageBand loops?

One night in October 2009, I got bored with the default loops that came with GarageBand. I figured the easy solution would be to download free GarageBand loops off the Web. To my surprise, I couldn't find one free site that had a large number of free GarageBand loops. Many websites advertised having free loops when in reality they sold most of their loops and only had a few free GarageBand loops. Thus, we provide free GarageBand loops (with no login required) so that other musicians won't feel my same frustration when looking to expand their reportoire of loops!
