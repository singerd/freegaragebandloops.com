Can I download GarageBand for Windows?!

No. Apple does not make a PC version of GarageBand. Why not, you may ask? Because Apple wants you to buy a Mac! GarageBand is part of the Operating System which comes with every Mac.


What GarageBand-like software exists for the PC?

Don't fret if you don't have GarageBand. The PC has many other cool options for music software. For example, before I had a Mac and used GarageBand, I used Fruity Loops on my PC. Please click here to learn more about Fruity Loops. Additional examples of music software for your PC that have similar functionality to GarageBand, are:

  • MixCraft - By Acoustica, MixCraft 5 is the current version. Click here to download a demo version of MixCraft. 

  • Cubase - By Steinberg, this comes in three versions: Cubase 5, Cubase Studio 5, and Cubase Essential 5. Click here to download trial versions of Cubase.

  • Reason - By Propeller Software. Click here to download a demo version of Reason.
