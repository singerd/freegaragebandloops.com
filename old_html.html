Why do we provide free GarageBand loops?

One night in October 2009, I got bored with the default loops that came with GarageBand. I figured the easy solution would be to download free GarageBand loops off the Web. To my surprise, I couldn't find one free site that had a large number of free GarageBand loops. Many websites advertised having free loops when in reality they sold most of their loops and only had a few free GarageBand loops. Thus, we provide free GarageBand loops so that other musicians won't feel my same frustration when looking to expand their reportoire of loops!

What is the vision of FreeGarageBandLoops.com?

The vision of this website is to provide the largest variety of cool, FREE GarageBand loops so that musicians everywhere can enjoy the thrill of making music.

Will this site get better?

Yes, if you're willing to share! Just like a peer-to-peer site, this site will work best if you give and take. At least it's good karma, too! :-)

Maybe you recorded yourself playing a bass track that you'd like to share with the world? E-mail it to us! Perhaps you captured a cool melody you played on the keyboard? Share it! As long as you name your file correctly then you will get credit for the loop (not that credit is important, although it is polite). Please make sure to fill out this form completely. If you don't know how to zip your loops into one folder then try reading these instuctions. Or e-mail us at help@freegaragebandloops.com and we'll walk you through it.
